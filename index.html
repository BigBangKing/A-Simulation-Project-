Virtual Driving Training Sessions

An Immersive University Project for Safe Driving Mastery

Session 1: Controlled Starts and Stops

Objectives:

  • Practice controlled starts and stops.
  • Proper stopping at stop signs and pedestrian crossings.
  • Smooth acceleration and deceleration.

Session 2: Roundabout Navigation

Objectives:

  • Understand who has the right of way in roundabouts.
  • Use indicators (turn signals) correctly when entering and exiting.
  • Practice safe merging and exiting.

Training Environment:

  • Simple roundabout layout with signs.
  • Optional traffic flow (cars or ghost vehicles).

Explore interactive simulations with real-time feedback to master roundabout maneuvers.

Session 3: Right of Way at Intersections

Objectives:

  • Learn to determine who has the priority at intersections.
  • Handle four-way intersections with varying signs.
  • React to oncoming traffic from multiple directions.

Training Environment:

  • Multiple road junctions with Stop, Yield, and No Entry signs.
  • Virtual instructor provides real-time feedback.

Dive into complex scenarios with AI-driven traffic for unparalleled learning.

Session 4: Pedestrian Crossing Awareness

Objectives:

  • Stop correctly before pedestrian crosswalks.
  • Recognize pedestrian behavior and anticipate crossing.
  • Handle crossings with or without traffic lights.

Training Environment:

  • Urban street with multiple pedestrian crossings.
  • NPC pedestrians with basic walking animations or triggers.

Engage with lifelike pedestrian interactions to enhance urban driving skills.
